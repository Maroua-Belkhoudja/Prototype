Frequently Asked Questions

Why hire freelancers?

With the growing shortage of skilled workers, it's increasingly difficult to find competent full-time staff. More and more companies are working with freelancers who offer their services online. Freelance platforms allow businesses to quickly and reliably find the right experts, helping them stay competitive in today's market. Conversely, more freelancers prefer finding work online due to less administrative burden and greater work opportunities.

What makes JobDZ different?

JobDz focuses on quality over quantity, with rigorous vetting of professionals. Our platform offers escrow payment protection, dedicated project managers for larger projects, and AI-powered matching to connect you with the perfect employer/job seeker for your specific needs.

How much does registration cost?

Creating an account on JobDZ is completely free for both clients and freelancers. We only charge a small service fee when you hire someone or complete a project.

How can I apply for a job i JobDZ?

After creating an account, you can post your JobDz's CV on Seekers page by clicking on 'Apply for a job'.

How can I put a job offer on JobDZ?

After creating an account, you can post your offer on Employers page by clicking on 'Add your offre'.
